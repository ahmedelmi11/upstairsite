    Let's talk about public sex education

    Among the biggest problems in the world is healthcare whether it is due to the quality being offered, the knowledge of practitioners or access for all segments of society. In many societies, access to accurate information on sexual health and sexuality in general is difficult if not impossible to access. The aim of this website is to offer free, evidence-based information on sexual health and sexuality in a non-judgmental way.

    We have divided up this website in several sections to go through the basic information on anatomy, consent, psychology, best practices, detailed information on sexual positions and techniques preferred according to height/size/popularity/etc. This is a continuous learning site and new material will be added as and when deemed vital.
